Name "The Mecca"
Manufacturer Winton Grimwade's, Stoke on Trent, England
Writing "The Mecca" Foot Warmer & Bed Bottle Reg.No. 571373 NOTE ITS MERITS Conventional shape for either of above purposes. Can be easily withdrawn by means of cord. Very strong and has no Knobs to break off. Made in finest English ivory Queens Ware.
Markings None

I do not know whether it was sold like this or is a display item for a shop. I also have a plain "Mecca" hot water bottle.
