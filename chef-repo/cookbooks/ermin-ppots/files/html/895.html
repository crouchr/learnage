Name The Gladstone Bag
Manufacturer Bourne, Denby, England
Writing B.E.D.
Markings
  • Registration Number 740416
  • Stitching effect around edges of bag.
  • Four "feet"
Notes A classic. Denby brought out a range of childrens water bottles of which the Gladstone Bag appears to be the most popular judging by the number on the market. The other shapes include a rabbit, an owl, a cat and another bag with some coloured parts. These are all highly, highly collectable not least by Denby collectors. If you see one for under £150 in good condition, then it's a bargain. The bag in the picture would have originally had a leather strap which is quite rare to find now.
