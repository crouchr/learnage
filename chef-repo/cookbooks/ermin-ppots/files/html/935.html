Notes This is obviously an up-market bottle, very light and made of china, not earthenware. I have seen these hot water bottles with shell-like shapes cast into the pottery, however when I tried to buy them, I was outbid by Royal Doulton collectors (boo hiss!), not by a hot water bottle collector - there is clearly a cross-over market for fine pieces.
