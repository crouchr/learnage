Name The Adaptable Water Bottle & Bed Warmer
Manufacturer The Old Fulham Pottery, London, England
Writing THE ADAPTABLE HOT WATER BOTTLE & BED WARMER. ALL WATER BOTTLES SHOULD BE WARMED BEFORE BEING FILLED WITH BOILING WATER
Markings
  • Boots logo on the back.
  • Number "3" indicating 3 pint capacity
Notes There are quite a lot of these around of various shapes and sizes, however the metal cap and the Boots early advertising makes this item rare. You see these items for sale with the incorrect top and without the wooden bead carrying handle. Do not purchase these particular items.
